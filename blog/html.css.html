How is Padding, Margins, and Borders like Furniture??

What do you need to know when you buy furnitre?

/>

First thing you need to know is the size of your place. This is what we would call the margins, or total area you can work with. You probably want to know if you can even get your chair into your place. In a website, this is your area to play and helps you decide the layout and rooms you want



Finally you want to move the furniture around inside the room like a jigsaw puzzle so everything fits. This would be called the padding and in a website, you can play within the border and add or move multiple bits like images, texts and lots of other fun things.



Next you need to understand the space you are working with. No point buying a large bed that won't fit inside the bedroom. This is the border of your room, or a specific section within the website that you are working inside. A large image won't work in a small and will look odd. Same thing as a bed will look funny if you can't fit any other things
