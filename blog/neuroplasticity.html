Neuroplasticity

We are never too old to learn things, and there is soemthing exciting about neuroplasticity and growth mindset in later years.
Firstly, our brain always has the ability to change regardless of physical and/or mental challenges. A good example is the ability for our brains to shift dominance from right-handed to left if we lose the use of the right.
Secondly, when you get older, your are learning because it is fun and you can learn anything you want!
