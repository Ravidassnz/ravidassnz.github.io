Te Whare Tapa Whā

taha tinana (physical wellbeing)

Go to the gym 2 times a week. Play outside with the kids at least once a week.

taha hinengaro (mental wellbeing)

Spend time cooking something from scratch and listen to an audiobook

taha wairua (spiritual wellbeing)

Be thankful each day of the oppotunities that I have been given over the years.

taha whānau (family wellbeing)

spend 3 days a week spending time with just the kids without distractions from devices

Whenua (Interconnections to the land and environment)

Take the kayak out and just float on the water
