How Human Skills has changed me

  • You can always learn new tools, be a better person, but you should also accept and appreciate your limitations. Nobody is perfect and don't beat yourself up about it.
  • Human skills (values, empathy and self-awareness) improves the dev community you belong to, and improves your own happiness and output.
  • My input is valued regardless of how new I am to coding.
  • The challenging part was revisiting weaknesses in myself that I know will take time to change or imrpove
  • Human skills is such an important part of dev work, where you need to work closely and dynamically across multiple teams. I can see why it is incredibly important throughout ones career and will ongoing
  • I don't think it is a waste of time to learn Human Skills, but am quite keen to apply this learning to bootcamp. I feel that this is where the theory will play a massive role in the practical side.
